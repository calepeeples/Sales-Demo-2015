How we choose

We rate all physicians based on factors that demonstrate clinical excellence such as patient outcomes, training, research, hospital affiliation, and procedure volumes.

We then find the top physicians that are located near you, accept your insurance, and satisfy any personal preferences.

Finally, your Staff Physician, a licensed doctor, will hand pick the specialist that best matches your needs.
