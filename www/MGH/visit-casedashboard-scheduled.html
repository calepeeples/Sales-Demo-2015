Guide to better visit

1. Bring an updated medication list

Bring to your appointment a list of all prescriptions, over-the-counter medicines, and vitamins/minerals that you take, along with dosage and frequency information. This allows your doctor to check for drug interactions before writing new prescriptions.

2. Bring a list of questions

Print and bring our list of suggested questions to ask your doctor during your appointment. Include any additional questions you have.

3. Take notes
