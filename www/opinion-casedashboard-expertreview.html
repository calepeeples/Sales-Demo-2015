About Dr. Wang
Dr. Wang is Chief of Orthopedic Spine Surgery, Professor of Orthopedic Surgery and Neurosurgery, and Co-Director of the USC Spine Center at USC School of Medicine. As an expert in the treatment of neck and back disorders, he has authored more than 100 research publications and has presented on the topic to audiences around the world. Dr. Wang sits on the Board of Directors for the North American Spine Society and holds several committee positions with the Cervical Spine Research Society and Scoliosis Research Society.
